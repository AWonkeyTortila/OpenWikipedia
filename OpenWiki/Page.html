OpenWikipedia

What is OpenWikipedia?

OpenWikipedia is a free, open sourced and light-weight encyclopedia, built from scratch, without any libraries, to be shared to all people.


What is OpenWikipedia available on?

Open Wiki is a website but also a downloadable project for offline uses. It is currently only available in English but further plans are made for French & Spanish.
